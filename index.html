Crystal Collector

You will be assigned a random number at the beginning of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score

You win by matching your total score to the random number; you lose if your score exceeds the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the value for each crystal.
